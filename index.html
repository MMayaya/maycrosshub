Teacher Transfer Matching, Built Around Real Educator Needs

May Cross Hub helps educators look for possible reciprocal transfer matches. The platform is designed to compare where an educator is currently posted, where they want to go, and whether another educator’s professional profile is compatible enough for a serious match request.

Important note: May Cross Hub can help educators discover possible matches, but official transfer rules, departmental procedures, school approval, and employer requirements must still be followed.

What May Cross Hub Is For

A clean way to find educators whose transfer needs cross with yours.

  • Educators register with verified email access before using the platform.
  • The system stores current post details, desired transfer details, and privacy preferences in Firebase.
  • Automatic matching looks for educators whose current location and desired location line up with another educator’s transfer route.
  • Users can decide whether to accept, ignore, or respond to match requests.
  • Sensitive details such as cellphone numbers and email addresses stay hidden by default.

Registration Overview

These details belong on the registration page, but the homepage explains what the platform will use for matching.

Basic Information

  • Full names: name and surname
  • Email address with verification before access
  • Cellphone number, not shown publicly
  • Password for secure sign in
  • Consent checkbox before registration

Current Post Details

  • School name, district, and province
  • Circuit or ward, optional
  • School quintile from 1 to 5
  • Phase taught: Foundation, Intermediate, Senior, or FET
  • Grades taught from 1 to 12
  • Main qualified subjects and additional current subjects
  • Post level or role for compatibility checks

Desired Transfer Details

  • Desired province, district, and town
  • Preferred school, optional
  • Willing to accept nearby areas: yes or no
  • Optional reason for transfer
  • Availability for match requests

Privacy Settings

These defaults follow a privacy-first approach while still allowing educators to see the professional details needed for matching.

Privacy option Recommended default Why it matters
Show my full name to matched educators Off Can be revealed after a match request is accepted.
Show my school name Later Useful for serious matches, but best hidden at first.
Show my subjects and district On Needed for matching without exposing private contact details.
Show my cellphone number Off Cellphone numbers should stay private unless the educator chooses otherwise.
Show my email address Off Match requests can happen inside the platform first.
Allow educators to send me match requests On Allows compatible educators to start a controlled request.
Allow admin to contact me about possible matches On Allows admin help where an important match may be missed.
